# 🐞 Bug Report: Escape Plane Floating – Cayo Perico Heist (GTA V Online)

> **Bug ID:** QA-003  
> **Game:** GTA V Online  
> **Mission:** Cayo Perico Heist – Intel Gathering  
> **Platform:** PC  
> **Mode:** Online  
> **Category:** Gameplay / Spawn / Physics  
> **Severity:** High  
> **Priority:** High  

---

## 🧾 Description
During the Intel Gathering mission in the Cayo Perico Heist, the escape plane occasionally spawns above ground level at the airstrip, making it inaccessible and preventing mission completion.

---

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Start Cayo Perico Heist – Intel Gathering mission  
2. Progress through objectives until reaching the escape phase  
3. Travel to the designated airstrip location  
4. Observe the spawn position of the escape plane  

---

## ✅ Expected Result
- Plane spawns correctly on the runway/ground  
- Player can access and board the aircraft  
- Mission proceeds normally  

---

## ❌ Actual Result
- Plane spawns floating above ground  
- No accessible path to reach the aircraft  
- Mission cannot be completed without restarting  

---

## 📊 Repro Rate
Occurs intermittently (~15–20% of mission attempts)  

---

## 🎯 Impact
- Blocks mission completion  
- Forces mission restart  
- Disrupts gameplay flow in a critical heist sequence  
- Negative player experience in high-value mission content  

---

## 🔍 Notes / Analysis
- Possible spawn point height miscalculation  
- Terrain or LOD loading inconsistency at airstrip  
- Potential network synchronization issue affecting object placement in online mode
